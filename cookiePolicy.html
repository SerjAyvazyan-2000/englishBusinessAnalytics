Cookie Policy

This policy explains how we use cookies on our website and how you can manage their usage.

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your device when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to remember your device and preferences, improving the user experience.

How we use cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: We use cookies to collect information about how you use our website to improve its functionality and content.
  • Personalization: We use cookies to remember your preferences and personalize your experience on our website.
  • Advertising: Some cookies are used to show you relevant advertisements both on our site and across other platforms.

Types of cookies we use

Our website may use the following types of cookies:

  • Necessary cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function correctly. Without them, certain website features may be unavailable.
  • Functional cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember your preferences (e.g., language or region) to improve your user experience.
  • Analytical cookies: These cookies collect information about how users interact with the website to help us improve its performance.
  • Advertising cookies: These cookies are used to display advertisements that may be of interest to you based on your previous interactions with the website.

How to manage cookies

You can manage cookie usage in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to control cookie preferences, including blocking or deleting them. However, blocking cookies may affect some features on our website.

Changes to our cookie policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the law or our practices regarding cookie usage. We encourage you to check this page periodically to stay informed of any updates.

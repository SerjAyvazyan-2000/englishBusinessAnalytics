Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you visit our website:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide through forms on our site.
  • Non-Personal Information: This includes data such as your browser type, IP address, device type, operating system, and usage patterns collected through cookies.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: We use your personal information to communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, and provide the services you requested.
  • To Improve Our Website: We use non-personal information to analyze trends and make improvements to the website’s functionality and user experience.
  • To Send Marketing Communications: If you opt-in, we may send you promotional emails about new courses, offers, and updates.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting business, such as email delivery and payment processing.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to legal requests.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track your activity on our website. Cookies help us improve the functionality of the site, remember your preferences, and provide a more personalized experience.

For more information, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we update or correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe link in our emails.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We recommend that you review this policy periodically.
